The deal damage attack effect menu can be used to configure a 'deal damage' attack effect. It should look like this:

Damage

The damage that will be dealt to the target. This must be specified in half hearts.

Delay

The delay between the time of the attack and the damage caused by this effect, in ticks. If the target of this attack effect is the attacker, you can simply use a delay of 1 tick, which will deal damage nearly right after the attack. If the target of this attack effect is the victim, you probably need a longer delay:

Minecraft entities can only take damage ~1 time per second. When they take damage, they become immune to future damage for ~1 second. Any attacks that hit during this time will be ignored completely. Since the victim was already damaged by the attack that triggered this attack effect, it will ignore the damage of this effect if the delay is too short.
